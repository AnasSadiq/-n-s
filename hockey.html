Hockey India, on Wednesday, announced a 16-member Indian men’s hockey team that will compete at the Paris 2024 Olympics, scheduled from July 26 to August 11.

Ace drag-flicker and defender Harmanpreet Singh will captain India at Paris 2024 while experienced midfielder Hardik Singh will serve as the vice-captain.

Harmanpreet is set to play in his third Olympics, having debuted as the youngest member of the Indian squad at Rio 2016 and subsequently playing a pivotal role in India’s historic bronze medal win at Tokyo 2020.

The squad also features veteran goalkeeper PR Sreejesh and midfielder Manpreet Singh, both of whom will be making their fourth Olympic appearance. Manpreet Singh was India’s captain at Tokyo 2020.

Five players, Jarmanpreet Singh, Sanjay, Raj Kumar Pal, Abhishek and Sukhjeet Singh, are set to make their Olympic debut in Paris.
