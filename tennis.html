A young crop of Indian badminton players will be in action at the Badminton Asia Junior Championships 2024 which will take place at the Among Rogo Sports Hall in Yogyakarta, Indonesia.

The badminton tournament will feature mixed team and individual events. The mixed team matches are scheduled from June 28 to July 2 while the individual events will take place from July 3 to July 7.

Tanvi Sharma, who reached the final of the Senior National Badminton Championship last year, will lead a young 18-member squad selected by the Badminton Association of India.

The 18-member Indian team includes All India Junior Ranking champions Pranay Shettigar and Aalisha Naik. Dhruv Negi and Navya Kanderi are also two players to keep an eye out for.

India find themselves in Group C of the mixed team championship where they will take on hosts Indonesia, Vietnam and the Philippines. The top two teams from each group will progress to the quarter-finals.
